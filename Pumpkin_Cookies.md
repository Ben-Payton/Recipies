# Ingredients
- 1/2 cup pumpkin
- 1/2 cup brown sugar

- 3/4 cup candied fruit
- 1 1/4 cup flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp flavor
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- 1 tsp allspice
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp grated lemon rind
- 3/4 cups chopped nuts
- 1/2 cup raisins
# Instructions
 - bake ate 350 F for 12 to 15 min
